Single Room

Conceived as the ultimate Santorini hideaway where blissful luxury meets holistic wellness, our two-storey, 265 square-metre Three-Bedroom Villa immerses guests in a contemporary haven of Cycladic sensibility, defined by lava rock, wood and water. On the lower level, a five-metre-long indoor heated swimming pool and relaxation area, indoor hot tub, Hammam and mini gym constitute your very own wellness haven. Two spacious living rooms, including a dining area with its own fully equipped kitchen, make the villa ideal for a cosy night-in, dinner party or entertaining, as do the state-of-the art lighting and SONOS wireless sound systems that allow guests to set the desired mood by creating their own colour scenarios and their favourite music throughout the Villa. The Villa’s sleeping quarters comprise a King size bedroom featuring a walk-in closet and bathroom with walk-in rain shower, as well as two Queen size bedrooms with en-suite bathrooms, also with walk-in showers. Rounding up the amenities, a private balcony perched high above the sea is the ideal spot to soak up the sun and enjoy romantic candlelit dinners and sunset drinks.

Suite Room

Our largest and most immersive offering, the Wellness Villa is the ultimate island escape. Designed with comfort, elegance and wellness in mind, the two-storey, 350 square-metre Villa is rich in amenities and therefore ideal for large families and groups of friends. Contemporary in aesthetic, it’s nevertheless imbued with Santorini’s spirit, embracing elements such as lava rock, wood and water. The cavernous spaces on the lower level are partly built into the volcanic caldera immersing guests in a haven of primal sophistication and effortless luxury. Three spacious living rooms, including a dining area with its own fully equipped kitchen, make the villa ideal for a night-in, dinner party or entertaining, as do the state-of-the art lighting and SONOS wireless sound systems that allow guests to set the desired mood by creating their own colour scenarios and play their favourite music throughout the villa. Private quarters are comprised of four bedrooms on the lower level, all complemented by walk-in closets and bathrooms with walk-in rain showers and designer amenities. Rounding up the villa’s amenities is a private wellness area with a five-metre-long indoor heated swimming pool with a relaxation area where you can enjoy body and facial treatments or a relaxing massage, a mini gym, an indoor hot tub and a Hammam. An additional Hot Tub enjoys stunning views of the Aegean Sea and Santorini’s world-famous sunset. Expansive views can also be enjoyed from the Villa’s two balconies which are equipped with sun loungers and a dining area, ideal for romantic candlelit dinners and sunset drinks.
